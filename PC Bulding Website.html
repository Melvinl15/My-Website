After you recieved all for components for your PC, all that left is the assembly. it may seem tricky at first but it is a lot simpler then you think. Some important notes to remember is first you dont need to push down on the CPU.
Once you lower the CPU into its socket it will fall in on it own. if you want to check that it in then give is a light wiggle. if nothing moves than your CPU is in its socket. On your Ram stick there will be a gap
line this gap with the indent on your motherboard to avoid bending any pins. if you are worried about static shock then you can purchase an anti-static pad with braclet to remove any excess charge you may have.


  1. take out your motherboard and locate the CPU socket and find the level next to it and unhook the lever
  2. Grab your CPU and Place it inside its socekt,then lower the lever back to secure you CPU
  3. Apply your CPU cooler(either stock or purchased) then connect cable to motherbord pins saying "CPU Fan"
  4. Find your RAM sticks and insert them into their socket
  5. Now you can place your motherboard inside your case (ensure that stand-offs are in correct place for the size of your motherboard)
